Dataset:  concussion.dat

Source:  T. Covassin, C.B. Swanik, M.L. Sachs (2003). "Sex Differences
and the Incidence of Concussions Among Collegiate Athletes", Journal
of Athletic Training, Vol. (38)3, pp238-244

Description:  Counts of Concussions among collegiate athletes in
5 sports for 3 years by gender.

Sport    9-27  
Academic Year   31-34
